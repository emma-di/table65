Post a meal · sign up to eat, cook, or clean

How it works

  • A head cook posts a meal — everyone can see it and sign up to eat, help cook, or clean.
  • Signups and meals are tied to the device you first used, so to edit or delete use the same device. To unsign up, just tap the same button again.
  • Signups close before the meal (set by the cook) so they know how much to buy.

House norms

  • If you sign up to eat, please also sign up to help cook or clean.
  • The head cook will let the group know the grocery cost and how to pay them back.
